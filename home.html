The beauty of the game is in its simplicity.

IP-SOCCER focuses on the technical side of the soccer game. Whether it be the technique of touch, pass, skill, finish or awareness, a strong technical base is huge in our training sessions. A player's individual game reaches a new dimension when they're technically strong.

IP-SOCCER is for soccer players ages 8-21, who are serious, dedicated and commited to develop the technical skills needed to raise their soccer game to a higher level.
